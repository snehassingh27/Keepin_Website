Features

One platform replaces your
entire item-tracking stack

More than just item storage — Keepin manages the full lifecycle of every item you own, from first scan to instant retrieval.

AI Visual Recognition — Zero Tags Required

Our patent-pending computer vision identifies items in real time from your phone camera alone. No barcodes, no stickers, no setup friction. Simply hold the item as you put it away and Keepin does the rest — a completely tag-free experience from day one. It even knows which numbered container you're placing items into automatically.

Conversational AI Retrieval

Ask in plain English — "Where's my red jacket?" Keepin responds like a personal assistant who was watching the whole time. Works with Alexa and Google Home too.

Virtual Rummage Mode

Scroll through a whole drawer or closet visually on your phone before physically searching. Browse shelf by shelf, box by box — without leaving your couch.

Smart Calendar & Weather Sync

Keepin checks your schedule and local forecast, then suggests what you'll need. Rain Tuesday? Umbrella reminder. Big hike Saturday? Gear list surfaces automatically.

Learns Your Preferences

AI that remembers what you love. Favorite boots you always wear with blue jeans? Keepin learns and surfaces the pairing automatically, getting smarter with every use.

Private & Encrypted

Everything you show Keepin is protected with advanced encryption. Nothing leaves your account without your permission. Shared access is fully controlled by you.
